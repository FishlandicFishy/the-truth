Turn on Web & App Activity?
Web & App Activity saves the things that you do on our sites, apps and services. This includes your searches and interactions with partners, as well as any associated information such as location and language.

If you use your device without an Internet connection, your data may still be saved in your account once you return online.

Not all services save this data in your account.

Any data saved helps us give you more personalized experiences across services, such as faster searches, better recommendations and useful ads.

This data may be saved and used in any service where you are signed in to give you more personalized experiences. Data older than three months will be automatically deleted. You can delete your data manually, change your auto-delete option, or stop saving data in your account settings.
